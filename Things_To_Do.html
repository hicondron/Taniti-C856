Things To Do

While everyone’s aware of our perfect weather, and you’ve most likely encountered plenty of pretty pictures of our beautiful beaches and gently swaying fofoti trees, you might be left wondering:
What are the top things to do in Taniti?

Tours

By land, sea, and even submarine, an organized group or individual sightseeing tour is a safe, authentic way for you and your family to explore every beautiful corner of Taniti.


Book A Tour Now
Dining

From traditional Dutch to Asian, African, and Spanish, dining options are as diverse and welcoming as our people.




Reservations
Nightlife

Come Explore Taniti's night life. Our beachfront lounges, exciting casinos, and lively clubs make it easy to sip cocktails, share stories, dance, and play until the sun rises again.

Golf

If you’re looking for the best golf vacation on the beach, Taniti is your place to be. Consistently ranking as one of the best golf destinations in the world, Taniti features two fantastic courses.
